Siamese Cat

This is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of Asian Cats.
It has distinct features like blue almond shaped eyes, a triangular head, large ears and a muscular body.

British Shorthair

This is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of British Cats.
It is medium sized with a round face, a teddy bear appearance and a calm easy-going personality.

Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds.
It is the oldest long-haired breed and is native to North America.

Persian Cat

This is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of Persia.
It is a medium to large cat with a sturdy body type, short thick legs and a broad chest.
They have a round head, wide set round eyes and a short muzzle.

Ragdoll

It is a large blue-eyed cat breed with an affectionate dog-like personality.
It is slow maturing and often not reaching their full size and coat color until they are four.
